Why Your Donation Matters

At Our Lady's Wailing House, we strive to honor Mother Mary by spreading her message of love, forgiveness, and intercession. From daily devotions to livestreaming prayer sessions for those who cannot join us in person, our platform serves as a light for those seeking spiritual guidance. But maintaining this mission comes with challenges. Your support can make all the difference.

GIVE ONLINE

Where Your Contribution Goes

  • Maintaining our livestreaming services requires advanced technology and substantial internet costs.
  • Creating and distributing prayer materials demands significant resources.
  • Keeping our website running smoothly involves hosting fees, updates, and security.
  • Operational costs, like electricity and local logistics, are also rising.
